🌍 Travel Tips

  • 🧳 Pack light and smart.
  • 💧 Stay hydrated and healthy.
  • 🌏 Respect local cultures.
  • 📸 Document your journey.
  • 📅 Plan your itinerary in advance.
  • 🚖 Learn basic local phrases.
  • 🔋 Carry a power bank always.
  • 🗺️ Keep digital & offline maps ready.
  • 🧼 Carry hygiene essentials.
  • 💸 Always have some local cash.

🛡️ Safety First

  • 🔐 Lock up your belongings in a safe.
  • 🚶‍♂️ Avoid walking alone at night.
  • 📍 Share your live location with family.
  • 🚑 Know the nearest hospital or clinic.
  • 📵 Be cautious when using public Wi-Fi.

📱 Must-Have Apps

  • 📍 Google Maps – Navigation
  • 🛫 Hopper – Flight Price Tracker
  • 🧳 PackPoint – Packing Lists
  • 💬 Google Translate – Language Help
  • 📞 WhatsApp – International Messaging
  • 🗺️ Maps.me – Offline Maps

🚨 Emergency Numbers

  • 🚓 Police: 112 or local number
  • 🚑 Ambulance: 911 / 112
  • 📞 Lost Passport: Contact your embassy
  • 🏨 Hotel Front Desk: Save their number
  • 🆘 SOS App: Use built-in Android/iOS feature
